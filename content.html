Armani Exchange — Art Direction

Brand Identity
A complete reinvention of the Armani Exchange visual identity, bringing a bold new direction to the brand while maintaining its iconic heritage. The project encompassed brand strategy, visual systems, and implementation across digital and physical touchpoints.
Client
Armani Exchange
Designer
Giannantonio De Maldé
Role
Art Director, Lead Designer
Date Completed
November 2024
Collaborators
Marco Rossi, Sofia Bianchi

The challenge was to redefine Armani Exchange's visual identity while preserving its recognized heritage and appeal to a younger audience that values authenticity and bold expression. The solution was a comprehensive identity system that combines sharp minimalism with dynamic elements, creating a visual language that feels both timeless and contemporary.


Working closely with the Armani team, we developed a system that extends across all touchpoints—from packaging and retail environments to digital platforms and campaigns. The result is a cohesive yet flexible identity that reinforces the brand's position in the market while opening new creative possibilities.

The typography system was carefully crafted to balance legibility with character. We selected a custom variation of a grotesque sans-serif as the primary typeface, complemented by a sharp serif for editorial contexts. This pairing creates a distinctive voice that stands out in the competitive fashion landscape.


The color palette draws from Armani's heritage while introducing vibrant accents that bring energy to applications. Deep navy and warm gray serve as foundations, with strategic use of vermilion and sage green as accent colors that create visual interest and hierarchy.

For the digital experience, we created a design system that translates the brand identity into interactive elements. The website and mobile app feature clean layouts with bold typography, intuitive navigation, and engaging micro-interactions that enhance the user experience while reinforcing the brand personality.


The photography direction emphasizes authenticity and emotional connection, with a documentary-style approach that captures genuine moments and expressions. This strategy aligns with the brand's values and resonates with the target audience's desire for truthful representation.

The success of this project lies in its ability to honor Armani Exchange's heritage while pushing the brand forward. The new identity has been implemented across all touchpoints, receiving positive feedback from both the client and the audience. Sales have increased by 22% in the quarter following the rebrand, and engagement metrics on digital platforms show significant improvement.


This project demonstrates how thoughtful design can transform a brand's perception while maintaining its core values and recognition. The systematic approach ensures consistency across all applications while providing the flexibility needed for a global fashion brand to stay relevant in a rapidly changing market.
